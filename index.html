About me

My work is all about understanding users and collaborating with teams to identify, define, and improve their experiences. I love streamlining processes and making them smarter, always aiming to balance innovative solutions with technical realities and limitations.

As a Product Designer, I thrive on bridging the gap between development teams, non-technical teams, and users. I map out their needs and guide them towards more creative solutions. I'm skilled at quick visualization and pixel-perfect prototyping, which helps me understand workflows and test new ideas quickly. My knowledge of HTML, CSS, and some frameworks adds versatility to my toolkit.

For over three years, I ran my own design agency, which was a transformative experience. It sharpened my design skills and offered a better understanding of client needs, leading to many meaningful and positive outcomes. This journey was both challenging and incredibly rewarding.
